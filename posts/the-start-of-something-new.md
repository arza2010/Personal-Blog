I am like most humans, heavily distracted by things that are going around me, and I realised that it is not the most harmful thing provided that it is channeled correctly. And hence, voila, I've started my own blog site!

Now, if I were to explain why I have come up with this space, that conversation would take multiple tangents before coming to the actual reason. But, one concrete reason I found was my love for sidequesting.

I have always loved an unconventional approach. Whether it be at work, or even just trying to fit luggage into a car in a way most people can't think of, I think it explores the mind to think of different angles and outcomes.

And in doing things unconventionally, I have often found sidequesting interesting because it keeps things exciting, and in a way also helps me understand certain aspects of the "main quest" that I would have never thought of otherwise.

I think you all must be thinking by now: why this blog? Why not just do something on LinkedIn where someone can engage with your content directly and hopefully engage with / grow your network?

![](/assets/images/untitled.jpeg)

While the platform does help me put myself out there, the main thing is the creative freedom I have and also the ability to learn while building this space. Yes, I might be sacrificing the reach the platform offers, but in the long term, I truly believe that this fits my goals better. This not only helps me put out content in a way I want to, but it also helps me learn about web design, CMS, AI, etc.

Next, what's so important to get you to read this?

Well, I am going to hold myself accountable (yes, you can beat me with a stick if I don't end up delivering) to put out genuine, non-AI-generated content and share my perspectives. I hope to regularise putting out this content and making sure that you all get to see what my "unconventional" takes are, and yes, hope to have some amazing conversations as well!

Hope to see you all here soon!
